Beginner Plan "Foundations"

Day 1

4 exercises
Full-body Strength Training

  • Squats (3x12)
  • Push-ups (3x10)
  • Glute Bridges (3x15)
  • Dumbbell Rows (3x12)

Day 2

4 exercises
Cardio + Core

  • 20-30 min brisk walk or light jog
  • Plank (3x30 sec)
  • Bicycle Crunches (3x15)
  • Leg Raises (3x10)

Day 3

2 exercises
Mobility & Stretching

  • Stretching or yoga (20-30 min)
  • Foam rolling (10-15 min)

Day 4

4 exercises
Upper Body Strength

  • Wall Push-ups (3x10)
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press (3x12)
  • Dumbbell Bicep Curls (3x12)
  • Tricep Dips (3x10)

Day 5

4 exercises
Lower Body Strength

  • Bodyweight Squats (3x15)
  • Lunges (3x12 each leg)
  • Glute Kickbacks (3x15 each leg)
  • Calf Raises (3x20)

Day 6

4 exercises
Cardio + Light Core

  • 20-30 min cycling or brisk walk
  • Side Plank (3x30 sec each side)
  • Russian Twists (3x20)
  • Leg Raises (3x12)

Day 7

2 exercises
Stretch & Foam Roll

  • Gentle yoga/stretching (20 min)
  • Foam rolling (10 min)

Day 8

4 exercises
Full-body Strength Training

  • Goblet Squats (3x12)
  • Push-ups (3x12)
  • Dumbbell Deadlifts (3x12)
  • Plank Rows (3x10)

Day 9

4 exercises
Cardio + Core

  • 20-30 min cycling or brisk walk
  • Side Plank (3x30 sec each side)
  • Russian Twists (3x15)
  • Mountain Climbers (3x20)

Day 10

2 exercises
Mobility & Stretching

  • Stretching and mobility work (20 min)
  • Foam rolling (10-15 min)

Day 11

4 exercises
Upper Body Strength

  • Incline Push-ups (3x12)
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raises (3x12)
  • Dumbbell Chest Press (3x12)
  • Dumbbell Bicep Curls (3x12)

Day 12

4 exercises
Lower Body Strength

  • Split Squats (3x12 each leg)
  • Step-ups (3x12 each leg)
  • Romanian Deadlifts (3x12)
  • Glute Bridges (3x15)
