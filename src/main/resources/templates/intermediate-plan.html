Intermediate Plan "Progression"

Day 1

4 exercises
Full-body Strength Training

  • Squats (4x12)
  • Bench Press or Chest Press (4x8)
  • Dumbbell Rows (4x10)
  • Plank (3x30 sec)

Day 2

5 exercises
HIIT + Core

  • 30 sec on, 30 sec off x 5 rounds:
    Jumping Jacks, Squat Jumps, Burpees
  • Russian Twists (3x20)
  • Bicycle Crunches (3x20)

Day 3

2 exercises
Yoga or Mobility Work

  • Yoga session (20-30 min)
  • Foam rolling (10-15 min)

Day 4

4 exercises
Upper Body Strength

  • Pull-ups (3x8) or Assisted Pull-ups
  • Push-ups (3x15)
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press (4x10)
  • Tricep Dips (3x12)

Day 5

4 exercises
Lower Body Strength + Plyometrics

  • Deadlifts (4x8)
  • Walking Lunges (4x12 each leg)
  • Box Jumps (3x10)
  • Glute Bridges (4x15)

Day 6

4 exercises
Cardio + Core

  • 30 min moderate cycling or running
  • Side Plank (3x30 sec each side)
  • Leg Raises (3x20)
  • Mountain Climbers (3x30 sec)

Day 7

2 exercises
Stretching & Mobility

  • Full-body stretch (20-30 min)
  • Foam rolling (10-15 min)

Day 8

4 exercises
Full-body Strength Training

  • Front Squats (4x8)
  • Bench Press (4x8)
  • Dumbbell Rows (4x8)
  • Russian Twists (3x20)

Day 9

4 exercises
HIIT + Core

  • Burpees (30sec x5)
  • Jump Squats (30sec x5)
  • Mountain Climbers (30sec x5)
  • Leg Raises (3x20)

Day 10

1 exercise
Yoga or Foam Rolling

  • Yoga or foam rolling (20-30 min)

Day 11

4 exercises
Upper Body Strength

  • Pull-ups (3x8)
  • Push-ups (3x20)
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press (4x10)
  • Lateral Raises (3x12)

Day 12

4 exercises
Lower Body Strength

  • Bulgarian Split Squats (4x10 each leg)
  • Deadlifts (4x10)
  • Box Jumps (3x12)
  • Calf Raises (3x20)
